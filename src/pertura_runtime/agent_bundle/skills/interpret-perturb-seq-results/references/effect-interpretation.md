# Effect Interpretation

## Expression

A replicated expression contrast supports an association within its explicit condition, state, and independent-unit scope. Direction and uncertainty are gene-specific; a global shift does not imply differential expression for every gene.

## Target reliability

Direct target response, guide consistency, responder fraction, and signature response answer related but different questions. A reliability caution limits interpretation of downstream effects even when an effect estimate completes.

## State and composition

A mapped-state change may reflect transcriptional movement within a reference, altered state abundance, selective survival, or assignment uncertainty. A composition association does not by itself establish fate conversion.

## Modules

A module score summarizes a gene set or learned program. Check how the module was learned and whether it used perturbation or evaluation labels. Module effects do not automatically establish a regulator or mechanism.

## Predictions

A virtual perturbation remains a prediction after comparison with measurements. Concordance evaluates a specified metric and scope; it does not convert the prediction into measured evidence.
